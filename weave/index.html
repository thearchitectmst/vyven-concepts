The idea behind the mark

VYVEN · pronounced "weaven"

The name comes from weaving — two distinct sets of threads interlaced into a single fabric. We do it with people. Different lives, different presences, converging into one shared moment.

The logo isn't drawn. It's generated — a parametric ribbon swept along a string-theory-inspired curve. Two edge curves (the two strings of experience) are offset from a single path and lofted together into one surface. That loft is the memory.

  • A 3D parametric center curve
  • Oscillatory radial & vertical modulation
  • A moving local frame with continuous twist
  • Two edge curves, lofted & thickened into the ribbon

Pull the Weave slider and the two strings draw together until they become the mark. Press Presence and the form dissolves into a field of living light — every dot a person, every person there. Thousands of perspectives. One shared moment.
